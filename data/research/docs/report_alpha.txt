Date:       Year 854, Day 221

Status:     ONGOING

SUBJECT OVERVIEW
────────────────
Specimen #001, designated "Sawney," captured during the
57th expedition beyond Wall Rose. Classified as a pure
titan. Estimated height: 14 metres.

BEHAVIORAL OBSERVATIONS
────────────────────────
Specimen shows no signs of intelligence or self-awareness.
Movement patterns are purely instinctual, driven by an
apparent biological imperative to consume humans.

No functional digestive system was observed — consumption
serves no metabolic purpose. Titans appear to absorb
sunlight for energy through an unknown mechanism.

PHYSICAL PROPERTIES
────────────────────
  - Regeneration rate : wounds heal within 1–3 seconds
  - Critical weakness  : nape of neck (approx. 7 cm²)
  - Internal organs    : vestigial or entirely absent
  - Body temperature   : significantly above human normal
  - Moisture output    : steam on injury and demise

NOTES
──────
Specimen escaped during the night of Day 221. A security
breach is under investigation. Second specimen "Bean"
also confirmed missing. Immediate lockdown of all
research archives has been recommended.

Deeper specimen data is located in the DELTA archive.
Access to the DELTA archive requires Level IV clearance.
